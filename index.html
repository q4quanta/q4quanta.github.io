Quantum Gates

Quantum Gates are fundamental building block of quantum circuits. There are single qubit gates, double and multiple qubits gates. These gates can be expressed as unitary matrices and qubits are expresses as column vectors.

Learn More

Quantum Circuits

Quantum circuit is a systematic execution of collection of quantum gates. A quantum circuit can be expressed as a Unitary matrix. In case single qubit circuit, it is a unitary matrix of size 2x2. As the dimention increases, size of matrix increases with size 2^nx2^n.

Learn More

Quantum Algorithms

A quantum circuit which performs a specific task is called quantum algorithm. Some example of quantum algorithms are: Quantum Fourier Transform (QFT), Grover's algorithm, HHL, QAOA, VQE.
